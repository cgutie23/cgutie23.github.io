Project Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide a professional platform for our 3D Designs, showcasing its services in 3D rendering, design, and blueprint creation for construction companies. The website will allow potential clients to learn about the business, view past work, and request design services.

Wireframe

Site Map

Page Design

  1. Home Page
    • Purpose: Introduce the company and its 3D design services
    • Users: Potential clients wanting to know more about the company
    • Content: Business history, team members, industry experience
    • Interactivity: Smooth scrolling to sections, rotating showcase of projects
  2. About page
    • Purpose: Provide background on Allstar Design Studio and its expertise.
    • Users: Potential clients wanting to know more about the company.
    • Content: Business history, team members, and industry experience.
    • Interactivity: Accordion sections: expandable sections about experience, technology used, etc.
  3. Services Page
    • Purpose: Detail the 3D design services offered.
    • Users: Construction companies, architects, real estate developers.
    • Content: List of services: 3D Modeling and rendering, Blueprint, Site plans and Virtual Walkthroughs.
    • Interactivity: Hover effects: Service descriptions appear on hover, and Forms: clients can select services and submit inquiries.
  4. Portfolio / Gallery Page
    • Pupose: Showcase past projects with high quality 3D renderings.
    • Content: High resolution images of 3D designs, Descriptions of each project
    • Interactivity: Gallery: Click to enlarge images in a slideshow format, Category: Filter projects by residential, commercial, and industrial.
  5. Contact Page
    • Purpose: Allow clients to reach out and request services.
    • Content: Contact form (name, email, project description, file upload), Business contact details.
    • Interactivity: Form Validation, google maps integration.

Dynamic Functionality

  • Image Sliders: showcasing completed 3D projects.
  • Form Validation: Ensuring proper user input in the contact form.
  • Dynamic Filters: Allowing users to filter services and projects.
  • Gallery Lighbox: Enlarging project images on click
  • Accordian Sections: Expanding / collapsing content for readability.

Conclusion

This website will serve as a professional digital portfolio for AllStar Design Studio, allowing construction businesses to explore services, view past projects, and request custom 3D designs. The combination of interactive features, JavaScript effects, and structured navigation ensures a modern, user-friendly experience.
